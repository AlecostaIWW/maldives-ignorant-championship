The Concept

An informal 10-day boat championship. No professional judges, no technical scoring. Just challenges, public humiliation, and undeserved glory.

Challenges have no fixed schedule — they activate when conditions allow, when someone calls one, or when the crew decides it's time. Some are active for the entire trip.

Daily Leader → must wear yellow speedo and yellow lycra for one session of their choice the following day.
Last Place → becomes Ship's Slave for the following 24 hours.

Permanent Surf Challenges

Active every day for the entire trip. Voted collectively at the end of each session. No self-votes.
First Barrel ONE TIME ONLY
First tube of the trip. Half a barrel counts. Ugly exit counts. Whoever gets it first keeps the points — and the right to talk about it for the remaining 10 days.
4pt
Best Wave of the Day DAILY
A clean maneuver, a decent line, a tube, or pure vision. Judged relative to the surfer's level. No self-votes.
2pt
Best Commitment DAILY
Biggest drop or most committed pull-in of the day. Crew judges.
2pt
Best Wipeout of the Day DAILY
Not who falls the most — who falls best. Style, impact, consequences. Reef contact scores double.
2pt
Best Claim DAILY
Independent of wave quality. The most absurd, unjustified, or aggressive claim of the day. Eye contact with the line-up is rewarded. Every tube automatically earns 1 claim point — crew decides how generous to be.
1pt
Best Eject DAILY
Exit from the board with the most style or most chaos. Nose bomb, somersault, involuntary flight. Crew judges.
1pt

Ship's Slave

Automatically assigned to last place each evening

  • Deliver coffee and breakfast to everyone before the morning session
  • Apply sunscreen to crewmates' backs
  • Rinse lycras after every session
  • Carry boards to the tenders
  • No complaining. Silence is part of the role.
  • No delegating.
  • Redemption: The Slave can challenge anyone on board. The person challenged picks the challenge. If the Slave wins, roles swap for 24 hours. If the Slave loses: −3 pt for the Slave, +3 pt for the other.

General Rules

  • 01No challenge is mandatory — but refusing too many will cost you reputation.
  • 02Disputes are resolved by majority vote. No appeals.
  • 03The yellow speedo is worn in the water. No exceptions for photos. Especially for photos.
  • 04The Ship's Slave cannot delegate duties. Silence is part of the role.
  • 05Surf votes are weighted by level. The criterion is relative courage, not absolute talent.
